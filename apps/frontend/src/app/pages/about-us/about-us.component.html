Our Story

Founded in 2020, Veltrix began with a simple idea: learning should be personalized, engaging, and data-driven.

Veltrix was born out of the challenges faced by students during the global shift to remote learning. Our founders, a team of educators and technologists, recognized that traditional educational tools weren't designed for the digital-first world we now live in.

Starting with a simple note-taking application, we quickly expanded to create a comprehensive learning platform that adapts to each student's unique needs and learning style. Our AI-powered study tools, collaborative features, and progress tracking have revolutionized how students approach their education.

Today, Veltrix is used by millions of students and educators worldwide, from high school students preparing for exams to university researchers collaborating on groundbreaking projects. We're proud to be at the forefront of educational technology, constantly innovating to make learning more effective, engaging, and accessible for everyone.
